## Profile

Wen Quan is an AI-assisted built environment researcher with a completed PhD in Interior Design from the School of Housing, Building and Planning, Universiti Sains Malaysia. His research connects age-friendly built environments, evidence-based design, public health resilience, spatial assessment, and applied AI methods. His published work has used LSTM-based model predictive control, natural language processing, SciBERT-assisted indicator extraction, contrastive learning, hyperbolic embedding, GANs, random forest regression, and multi-criteria assessment methods.

For the proposed second PhD in Computer Science and Technology, his research goal is to shift from domain-specific AI applications toward computational methods for knowledge-guided vision-language models and lightweight spatial intelligence in age-friendly indoor and community environment assessment.
